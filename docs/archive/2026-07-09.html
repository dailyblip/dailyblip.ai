Wednesday, July 8 — six things creators need to know

Issue 192 · 2026-07-09

The brief

1. Meta launched Muse — an image model that works from your own photo library. Restoration, style transfer, product shots; aimed straight at Firefly and Imagen.
2. Sora is sunsetting: the app closed in April and the API dies September 24. Eleven weeks to migrate any pipeline that touches it.
3. Midjourney V8.1 is now the default — native 2K, 4–5x faster, and a Draft Mode for cheap ideation before final renders.
4. July video rankings: Runway Gen-4.5 for control, Veo 3.1 for realism, Gemini Omni Flash at $0.10/sec for fast iteration. Pick by workflow, not leaderboard.
5. Copyright clarity is forming: AI-assisted work is protectable in many places if you can show human direction. Start keeping your prompt and edit history.
6. Fable 5 goes pay-per-use today; Sonnet 5 and Opus 4.8 stay bundled — writers, check which model your stack defaults to.

The month's big comparison crowns Runway Gen-4.5 the pro-workflow pick — camera direction, structured prompting, music videos and previs — while Veo 3.1 and Seedance 2.5 lead on raw realism. Kling 3.0 Turbo and Pika stay the daily drivers.
