15 ChatGPT Image Prompts for Scroll-Stopping Social Media Posts

Ready-to-use prompts for product shots, announcements, carousels, and branded graphics.

Last reviewed July 20, 2026
Image GenerationGetting Started

Fifteen prompts, organized by what you're actually trying to post. Copy one directly into ChatGPT and adjust the specifics to your own brand.

Prompts to try

Copy any of these directly, then swap in your own product, colors, or message.

PROMPTS TO TRY
Clean product shot
A skincare bottle on a marble surface, soft natural light from a window, minimal shadow, product photography, square format
Lifestyle product scene
A hand holding a coffee cup on a wooden cafe table, blurred background, warm morning light, lifestyle photography style
Announcement graphic
A clean announcement graphic with text reading "WE'RE BACK" in bold serif letters, soft gradient background in brand teal and amber, centered layout
Before/after style split
A vertical split-screen image, left side muted and dull, right side bright and vibrant, same subject on both sides, clear visual contrast
Carousel slide 1 of a set
A simple educational graphic with the text "Tip #1" in the top corner, minimal icon illustration below, clean flat background, consistent brand colors
Flat lay product arrangement
An overhead flat lay of skincare products arranged on a neutral linen background, soft even lighting, negative space for text overlay
Bold quote graphic
A bold typography graphic with the quote "Consistency beats perfection" in large white text on a solid deep navy background, centered
Behind-the-scenes style
A candid-style photo of hands working at a desk with a laptop and notebook, natural window light, warm and approachable, not overly polished
Countdown/urgency graphic
A bold graphic with text reading "48 HOURS LEFT" in large condensed lettering, high contrast background, sense of urgency without clutter
Founder or team portrait style
A friendly portrait of a person at a desk, soft natural light, genuine smile, neutral background, approachable and professional
Product in use
A close-up of hands applying a skincare product to skin, soft diffused lighting, clean and clinical but warm, shallow depth of field
Seasonal themed graphic
A cozy autumn-themed flat lay with a candle, warm blanket texture, and a coffee cup, soft warm lighting, muted seasonal color palette
Comparison graphic
A simple two-column comparison graphic, left labeled 'Before' and right labeled 'After', minimal icons, clean sans-serif text, brand colors
Testimonial background
A soft blurred abstract background in brand colors, gentle gradient, plenty of empty space in the center for a text overlay
Bold statement portrait
A dramatic portrait with strong side lighting, deep shadows, high contrast, moody and confident expression, editorial style
KEY TAKEAWAYS
  • Specific product and background details beat generic style words every time.
  • Put any exact text you need in quotes so it actually renders.
  • State the platform's aspect ratio need (square, vertical, wide) directly in the prompt.
