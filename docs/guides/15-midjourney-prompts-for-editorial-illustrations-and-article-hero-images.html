15 Midjourney Prompts for Editorial Illustrations and Article Hero Images

Clean, composed images for blogs, newsletters, and explainer articles.

Last reviewed July 20, 2026
Image GenerationTools & Workflow

Editorial images need to read clearly at a glance, not just look impressive up close. These fifteen are built for that job specifically.

Prompts to try

Built for wide article-header framing, with a single clear focal point.

A hero image only has to do one job: make someone want to keep reading. These are built for exactly that.

KEY TAKEAWAYS
  • Editorial images need one clear focal point, not a busy scene.
  • Wide aspect ratios like 16:9 fit most article header slots directly.
  • Lower stylize values keep the image readable rather than overly abstract.
