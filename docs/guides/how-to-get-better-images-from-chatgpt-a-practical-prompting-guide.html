How to Get Better Images From ChatGPT: A Practical Prompting Guide

Describe composition, lighting, and style like a creative director, not a search query.

Last reviewed July 20, 2026
Image GenerationGetting Started

Vague prompts get vague images. ChatGPT's image model (GPT Image) rewards specifics the same way a real photo shoot does: subject, style, lighting, framing.

Describe it like a creative brief

Name the subject clearly, then add style, mood, and lighting on top of it.

"A golden retriever" gives the model almost nothing to work with. "A golden retriever sitting in a sunlit kitchen, warm morning light, shallow depth of field" gives it an actual scene.

Lighting direction matters more than most people expect. "Soft window light from the left" changes the image more than most style words do.

Put exact text in quotes

GPT Image is genuinely good at rendering readable text, which makes it useful for posters, signage, and social graphics.

But it only renders text reliably if you put it in quotes. Unquoted text is treated as a suggestion, not a hard requirement.

Write it as: text reading "SUMMER SALE" in bold white letters, not just summer sale text on the image.

None of this requires technical skill. It's the same instinct a photographer already has: name the subject, then direct the scene around it.

PROMPTS TO TRY
Product on a clean background
A ceramic coffee mug on a plain white background, soft studio lighting from the upper left, subtle shadow, product photography style
Portrait with directional light
A close-up portrait of a woman smiling, warm golden hour light from the left side, shallow depth of field, natural skin texture
Text-forward social graphic
A minimalist poster with the text "NEW ARRIVALS" in bold black letters, centered, clean beige background, plenty of negative space
KEY TAKEAWAYS
  • Describe subject, style, and lighting separately, not as one vague sentence.
  • Lighting direction changes results more than most style keywords do.
  • Put exact text in quotes, or it won't render reliably.
